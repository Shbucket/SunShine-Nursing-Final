Who We Are

Sunshine Nursing is a Naples-based, private nursing service offering customized care to clients in the comfort of their own home. We understand that our service is only as good as our caregivers. So here at Sunshine Nursing we strive to hire the most respectful, compassionate, caring, and loving individuals. We want to give your loved ones the most assistance as possible while not disrupting their daily routine. We promise to deliver outstanding health care services and health care-related services to meet the clients personal needs. Our nurses will assess the client and then develop a personalized care plan for your loved ones, based off of their unique traits and needs. Sunshine Nursing is dedicated to providing the highest quality care and comfort all within the home. Our services are provided by licensed RNs, BSNs, LPNs certified and familiar with the local area.
